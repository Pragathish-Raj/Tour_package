Paris Romantic Getaway

Experience the magic of Paris with your loved one on this unforgettable 5-day journey through the City of Love.

Package Highlights

Romantic Seine River Cruise

Enjoy a gourmet dinner with live music as you glide past Paris's illuminated landmarks.

Eiffel Tower Summit Experience

Champagne toast at the summit with breathtaking views of Paris.

Champagne Region Day Trip

Visit prestigious champagne houses and enjoy tastings in Reims.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Romantic Seine Cruise

  • Airport transfer to your 4/5-star boutique hotel
  • Evening: Seine River Dinner Cruise with gourmet dinner & live music

Day 2: Iconic Paris – Eiffel Tower & Montmartre

  • Breakfast at Café de Flore (historic Parisian café)
  • Eiffel Tower Summit Access with champagne toast
  • Stroll through Trocadéro Gardens for the best photo ops
  • Explore Montmartre (artist square, Sacré-Cœur sunset views)
  • Romantic dinner at Le Consulat or La Maison Rose

Day 3: Art & Culture – Louvre & Champs-Élysées

  • Private Louvre Tour (see Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo without crowds)
  • Lunch near Palais Royal or at Angelina (famous hot chocolate)
  • Shopping & walk along Champs-Élysées + Arc de Triomphe
  • Optional: Moulin Rouge Show (extra cost)

Day 4: Day Trip to Champagne Region

  • Visit Reims (Moët & Chandon or Taittinger Champagne cellars)
  • Guided tasting & romantic picnic in the vineyards
  • Return to Paris for a free evening

Day 5: Le Marais & Farewell

  • Explore Le Marais (quaint streets, vintage shops)
  • Visit Place des Vosges (Paris' oldest square)
  • Farewell lunch at Chez Julien (romantic riverside bistro)
  • Airport transfer

Package Inclusions

  • 4 nights in a luxury boutique hotel (double occupancy)
  • Daily breakfast + 2 romantic dinners
  • All entry tickets (Eiffel Tower, Louvre, Champagne tour)
  • Private transfers (airport & tours)
  • Seine River cruise with dinner
  • 24/7 concierge service

Exclusions

  • Airfare & travel insurance
  • Personal expenses & optional activities
  • Meals not specified in the itinerary
