Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Seminyak Beach

  • Airport transfer to your 4/5-star resort
  • Evening: Sunset at Seminyak Beach & welcome dinner

Day 2: Ubud Cultural Tour

  • Tegallalang Rice Terraces visit
  • Sacred Monkey Forest exploration
  • Traditional Balinese dance performance
  • Art market shopping

Day 3: Mount Batur Adventure

  • Early morning trek to Mount Batur summit
  • Breakfast with volcano views
  • Relax at Toya Devasya Hot Springs
  • Free afternoon at resort

Day 4: Waterfalls & Temples

  • Tegenungan Waterfall visit
  • Goa Gajah (Elephant Cave) exploration
  • Besakih Temple tour (Bali's Mother Temple)

Day 5: Nusa Penida Island

  • Speedboat to Nusa Penida
  • Visit Kelingking Beach & Angel's Billabong
  • Snorkeling at Crystal Bay

Day 6: Uluwatu & Jimbaran

  • Uluwatu Temple & Kecak fire dance
  • Beach club relaxation
  • Romantic seafood dinner at Jimbaran Bay

Day 7: Departure

  • Spa treatment before flight
  • Airport transfer
