Discover Japan

Experience the perfect blend of ancient traditions and modern innovation on this 10-day journey through Japan's most iconic destinations.

Book Now

Package Highlights

Tokyo Metropolis

Explore the neon-lit streets of Shibuya, Shinjuku, and Akihabara.

Kyoto Temples

Visit Fushimi Inari's thousand torii gates and Kinkaku-ji's golden pavilion.

Mount Fuji

Experience the iconic mountain from Hakone's hot springs and Lake Ashi.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1-2: Arrival in Tokyo

  • Airport transfer to your hotel in central Tokyo
  • Evening: Welcome dinner at a traditional izakaya
  • Full day Tokyo tour: Meiji Shrine, Shibuya Crossing, Akihabara
  • Tsukiji Outer Market visit and sushi breakfast

Day 3-4: Hakone & Mount Fuji

  • Bullet train to Hakone
  • Pirate ship cruise on Lake Ashi with Fuji views
  • Onsen (hot spring) experience at ryokan
  • Traditional kaiseki dinner

Day 5-7: Kyoto

  • Bullet train to Kyoto
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine hike
  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) and Ryoan-ji temple
  • Gion district walking tour (geisha spotting)
  • Day trip to Nara (Todaiji Temple and deer park)

Day 8-9: Osaka

  • Train to Osaka
  • Osaka Castle visit
  • Dotonbori street food tour
  • Universal Studios Japan (optional)

Day 10: Return to Tokyo & Departure

  • Last minute shopping in Ginza
  • Airport transfer
