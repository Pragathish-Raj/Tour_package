Egyptian Adventure

Journey through 5,000 years of history on this 8-day expedition to Egypt's greatest ancient wonders.

Book Now

Package Highlights

Great Pyramids & Sphinx

Private tour of Giza Plateau with camel ride and pyramid interior access.

Luxor's Ancient Temples

Explore Karnak, Luxor Temple, and Valley of the Kings with Egyptologist guide.

Nile River Cruise

3-night luxury cruise from Luxor to Aswan with guided shore excursions.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Cairo

  • Airport transfer to 5-star hotel near Giza
  • Welcome dinner with pyramid view

Day 2: Giza Plateau & Memphis

  • Private tour of Great Pyramids and Sphinx
  • Camel ride around the pyramids
  • Entry to Khufu's Pyramid interior
  • Visit ancient capital Memphis and Step Pyramid of Djoser

Day 3: Egyptian Museum & Fly to Luxor

  • Tour Egyptian Museum (Tutankhamun's treasures)
  • Flight to Luxor
  • Evening sound and light show at Karnak Temple

Day 4: Luxor West Bank

  • Valley of the Kings (3 tomb entries included)
  • Temple of Hatshepsut
  • Colossi of Memnon
  • Board Nile cruise ship

Day 5: Nile Cruise - Edfu & Kom Ombo

  • Morning visit to Edfu Temple (Horus)
  • Afternoon visit to Kom Ombo Temple
  • Evening galabeya party on board

Day 6: Aswan Highlights

  • Philae Temple boat trip
  • Unfinished Obelisk
  • Optional Abu Simbel excursion (extra)

Day 7: Return to Cairo

  • Flight back to Cairo
  • Free afternoon for Khan el-Khalili bazaar shopping
  • Farewell dinner cruise on the Nile

Day 8: Departure

  • Transfer to airport

Package Inclusions

  • 7 nights accommodation (5-star hotels & cruise)

  • Domestic flights (Cairo-Luxor, Aswan-Cairo)
  • 3-night Nile cruise with all meals
  • Licensed Egyptologist guide throughout
